FAQ ?

1. What is Snap Stage?

Snap Stage is a registered micro enterprise that offers services in advertising, photography, travel planning, educational support, social work, creative arts, and personal services.

2. What kind of advertising services do you provide?

We handle both digital and offline advertising, including content creation, campaign planning, social media marketing, and market research.

3. Do you offer event photography or only commercial shoots?

We offer both. Our team covers weddings, birthdays, corporate events, as well as branding and product shoots.

4. Can I book a complete tour package through Snap Stage?

Yes, we provide complete travel agency services including ticket booking, hotel reservations, and customized tour plans.

5. What educational support services are available?

We provide online and offline tutoring, study materials, career guidance sessions, and training workshops for students.

6. What kind of social work does Snap Stage do?

We engage in non-residential social activities like awareness campaigns, youth skill development, and support for underprivileged communities.

7. Are you involved in any entertainment services?

Yes, we organize cultural events, talent showcases, and digital media productions involving music, drama, and arts.

8. What are “personal services” in your offerings?

These include event coordination, marriage bureau services, and other customized client-focused services.

9. Is Snap Stage officially registered?

Yes, Snap Stage is officially registered under the Udyam MSME portal
